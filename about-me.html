About Me

Yuan-Wei Pi.

I have been living in Berlin since October 2017 and currently work as an electrical engineer. I believe it was God who called me to this place.

Formation

Pieces of a larger puzzle.

Since 2023, He has been giving me pieces of a larger puzzle, gradually revealing the bigger picture. I’m grateful that He hasn’t shown me exactly what to do in the future. Instead, He has been shaping my character through various encounters, stories, and seasons of conflict, disappointment, misunderstanding, financial crisis, and even long suffering.

Through all of this, I believe He is keeping me humble and teaching me to rely on His grace alone—and He continues to do so.

Time and again, He has brought the right people into my life—people with whom I can pray, share life, and experience His great love and faithfulness. Some of us even share the same vision: to pray for the revival of this city. And I continue to ask Him for more.

Grace in Weakness

Learning through sickness and systems.

I also appreciate that He allowed me to experience sickness—not as a punishment, but as a way to better understand Berlin’s medical system. Through it, I became more aware of the limitations within both the healthcare system and the broader social structures.

And I’m deeply thankful that He has given me the grace to even appreciate my sickness.

What has become most precious to me is the growing realization of my own imperfection—and of His perfectness. I pray that I may continue to be someone after God's own heart.

"The One who calls you is faithful, and he will do it."

1 Thessalonians 5:24
