Proof of Concept

A Step Forward PoC.

Creating work pathways rather than relying only on aid.

Overview

Skill-building, training, and pathways to employment.

The initiative brings together local businesses, homeless participants, and supporting organizations so participants can gain practical skills for real work.

The pilot tests the model, learns what works, and refines it for broader implementation.

Current status: evaluating the plan with professionals and reaching out to organizations.

One-Page Description

The model connects opportunity with support.

01

The Idea

Test a model that connects business partners, homeless participants, and supporting organizations.

02

How It Works

Businesses train participants, and supporting organizations help recruit and connect people to the program.

03

Why a PoC?

This first stage tests training formats, useful skills, essential structures, and sustainable success measures.

Pilot Needs and Goals

Practical, measurable, scalable.

01

Funding Needs

Funding supports a small training space, coordinators or trainers where needed, a simple platform, and a pilot course in one or two skill areas.

02

Impact Goals

Train the first 5-10 homeless participants, engage at least 3-5 business partners, measure outcomes, and refine the model for a larger rollout.

03

Why Support This?

Supporting this PoC means helping a real-world test of a model that could transform how homeless people are empowered toward independence.
