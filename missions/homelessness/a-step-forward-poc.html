Proof of Concept

A Step Forward PoC.

A new approach to addressing homelessness by creating opportunities rather than relying solely on aid.

Overview

Skill-building, training, and pathways to employment.

The initiative brings together local businesses, homeless participants, and supporting organizations on one platform that enables skill-building, training, and pathways to employment.

Through partnerships with small businesses and service providers, participants gain practical skills that can be applied in real-world positions. Supporting organizations help connect individuals to the program, while a simple platform ensures smooth collaboration.

The aim of this pilot is to test the model, learn what works best, and refine it for broader implementation.

Current status: evaluating the plan with professionals and reaching out to organizations.

One-Page Description

The model connects opportunity with support.

01

The Idea

Homelessness can be tackled not only with aid, but with opportunity. The PoC tests a model connecting business partners, homeless participants, and supporting organizations.

02

How It Works

Business partners volunteer or invest time to train participants, participants gain practical skills, and supporting organizations help recruit and connect individuals to the program.

03

Why a PoC?

This first stage tests what kind of training works, which skills are most valuable, what structures are essential, and how success can be defined sustainably.

Pilot Needs and Goals

Practical, measurable, scalable.

01

Funding Needs

Funding supports a small training space, coordinators or trainers where needed, a simple platform, and a pilot course in one or two skill areas.

02

Impact Goals

Train the first 5-10 homeless participants, engage at least 3-5 business partners, measure outcomes, and refine the model for a larger rollout.

03

Why Support This?

Supporting this PoC means helping a real-world test of a model that could transform how homeless people are empowered toward independence.
