Action

Knowledge Hub.

A shared platform for guidance, support, and compassionate action.

Overview

Accessible information for those in need and those who want to help.

Inspired by Crisis homelessness charity and a friend in Portugal who prints practical support information, this project gathers trusted resources in one place.

The hub helps vulnerable people find services and helps churches, organizations, and individuals know how to respond.

Current status: gathering and structuring relevant information, and developing the foundational infrastructure for the platform.

Open live Knowledge Hub

Core Objectives

Support, guidance, consolidation, communication, and local engagement.

01

Accessible Support

Present clear, up-to-date resources for people in vulnerable situations.

02

Effective Help

Guide individuals, churches, and organizations toward practical ways to help.

03

Shared Platform

Bring outreach information together, reduce duplicated effort, and bridge communication gaps.

Action Points

Mapping, content, and platform development.

01

Resource Mapping

Identify local services, connect with frontline workers, and gather insights so the hub reflects real needs and trusted support options.

02

Content Creation

Develop clear, accessible materials, both printable and digital, including multilingual and localized guides.

03

Platform Development

Build a user-friendly platform that consolidates existing initiatives and allows easy updates, filtering, and community submissions.

Long-Term Impact

A trusted bridge between need and help.

By simplifying access to essential information, the platform can reduce confusion and make support more visible, connected, and actionable.
