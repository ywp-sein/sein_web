Action

Knowledge Hub.

A shared platform for guidance, support, and compassionate action for helping Berlin homeless.

Overview

Accessible information for those in need and those who want to help.

Inspired by Crisis homelessness charity and a dear friend in Portugal who regularly supports the homeless by printing and sharing helpful information, this project seeks to create a centralized Knowledge Hub.

Its aim is twofold: to provide essential, accessible information for people in vulnerable situations, such as the homeless, and to guide those who want to help with practical ways to do so.

More than just a resource list, this hub consolidates information from various initiatives, making it easier to navigate support systems, access services, and foster compassionate action.

Current status: gathering and structuring relevant information, and developing the foundational infrastructure for the platform.

Core Objectives

Support, guidance, consolidation, communication, and local engagement.

01

Accessible Support

Collect and present clear, up-to-date resources for individuals in vulnerable situations so they know where and how to get help.

02

Effective Help

Offer practical guidance and resources for individuals, churches, and organizations who want to support those in need but do not know where to start.

03

Shared Platform

Bring together information from various outreach projects, maximize visibility, reduce duplicated effort, and bridge communication gaps.

Action Points

Mapping, content, and platform development.

01

Resource Mapping

Identify local services, connect with frontline workers, and gather insights so the hub reflects real needs and trusted support options.

02

Content Creation

Develop clear, accessible materials, both printable and digital, including multilingual and localized guides.

03

Platform Development

Build a user-friendly platform that consolidates existing initiatives and allows easy updates, filtering, and community submissions.

Long-Term Impact

A trusted bridge between need and help.

The Knowledge Hub aims to become a sustainable and trusted bridge between those in need and those willing to help. By centralizing and simplifying access to essential information, the platform can reduce isolation and confusion for people in crisis.

Over time, it can foster a more connected and compassionate community, where support is not only more visible but also more actionable.
