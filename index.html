About SEiN

Anchored in love, led by the Spirit, for the glory of God.

The initiative offers a holistic, faith-based response where increasing social needs confront limited public resources. Inspired by George Mueller's faith and William Wilberforce's advocacy, SEiN combines ministry, Christian social education, business creation, and policy engagement.

Each person is created in God's image, uniquely designed with purpose.

Information is not the same as knowledge, and knowledge is not the same as wisdom.

Berlin needs people who make wise decisions and act with integrity.

Core Focus

Three strands, one calling.

01

Understanding

Study the causes, mindsets, behaviors, and systems around homelessness while remembering that every person is made in the image of God.

02

Education & Business

Build discipleship-first education with practical skills for work, independent living, and social businesses that restore dignity.

03

Social Advocacy

Address gaps in housing and healthcare through partnerships, public knowledge, prayerful truth-telling, and compassionate policy work.
