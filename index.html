Save Everyone in Need

Whenever an issue arises, we shall confront it without delay. Our allegiance is to justice and compassion, so that the poor are fed, injustice is opposed, and the weak are protected. And that change does not begin with governments or powers, but with us — with the awakening of conscience, the courage to love and act, and the transformation of each individual heart.

About SEiN

The awakening of conscience, the courage to love and act.

SEiN does not belong to the divisions of left or right, liberal or conservative, nor to any ideology or system. Wherever the poor are neglected, injustice is ignored, or the weak are left unprotected, change is not optional; it is imperative.

The poor are fed.

Core Focus

The applied method for each mission.

01

Understanding

Understand every aspect of a social issue: its causes, affected people, current mindsets and behaviors, and the systems around it.

02

Education & Business

Build discipleship-first education and practical pathways for work, independent living, restoration, and meaningful social business.

03

Social Advocacy

Address systemic gaps through partnerships, public knowledge, prayerful truth-telling, and compassionate policy engagement.

Current Mission

Homelessness is one social issue being confronted and ended.

The first mission focuses on homelessness in Berlin through field learning, healthcare and housing questions, prayer, practical education, and compassionate advocacy. Future missions can follow the same pattern: understand, confront, pray, and act.
