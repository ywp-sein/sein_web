About SEiN

Anchored in love, led by the Spirit, for the glory of God.

SEiN is a Christian education, business, and social initiative for Berlin's homeless. It begins with prayer and moves toward practical service, careful research, and dignifying pathways for restoration.

Why it exists

Berlin has deep social needs, and many people live at the edge of housing, healthcare, work, and community. SEiN responds by bringing spiritual formation, practical education, social business, and advocacy into one coherent work.

What shapes it

The initiative is inspired by faithful dependence like George Mueller and public courage like William Wilberforce. The aim is not simply to gather information, but to grow in wisdom and act with integrity.

Convictions
